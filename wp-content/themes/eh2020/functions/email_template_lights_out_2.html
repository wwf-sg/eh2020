Hi there,

A huge thank you for participating in the symbolic global Earth Hour lights out which took place on 28 March 2020 in 180 countries and territories around the world. Together, we stood united in our call to protect nature - and our future. This could not have happened without your support.

This is just the first step in your journey. The movement does not stop here, it goes beyond the hour.

2020 is a unique and critical time for us.

In 2020, world leaders have the opportunity to set the environmental agenda for the next decade and more. Crucial political decisions will be made on climate and biodiversity - decisions that will directly affect the fate of humanity, our planet, you and me for years to come.

Go one step further. Speak up and lend your voice to empower more decision makers in Singapore - our policymakers, businesses and institutions - to implement policies and practices for a better future.

RAISE YOUR VOICE

If you are a business representative: Speak to our Corporate Partnerships team at mtolentino@wwf.sg to understand how businesses can play a part in addressing this existential threat

If you are a grassroot organisation representative: Speak to our community team at volunteer@wwf.sg to understand how communities can come together to mitigate this threat

If you are a school representative: Speak to our education team at edu@wwf.sg to understand how schools can take action for nature.

The task is ambitious but when have we ever let that stop us?

Our future depends on the decisions we make now. Let's continue to work together and plant the seeds for a liveable and prosperous future.
