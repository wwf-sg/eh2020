Thank you for signing up to be part of the iconic Earth Hour lights out on 28 March from 8:30pm - 9:30pm!

By turning off your lights, you are taking a stand for environmental change. And when we do it together across Singapore, it becomes a symbol of unity. A symbol of hope. A symbol of power in collective action.

To know more about why we need to act and how you can be a part of the change, join Nathan Hartono, Benjamin Kheng, RRILEY, Paul Foster and many others on a special Earth Hour live stream at www.earthhour.sg on 28 March 2020, 5.30pm - 8.30pm. The digital event will culminate in the symbolic lights out at 8.30pm.

If you'd like to capture your Earth Hour lights out or activities, please refer to the guidelines here on photography and videography designed to help you and your team shoot effectively.

After the event, send your photos and videos to us at volunteer@wwf.sg or + 65 8488 1607 for compilation by 31 March.
